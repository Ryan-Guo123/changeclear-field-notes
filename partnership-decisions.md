# ChangeClear for Partnership Decisions

Partnership questions are rarely just about equity math.

They often involve:

- trust
- decision rights
- contribution
- commitment
- exit paths
- what happens when pressure changes the relationship

ChangeClear does not provide legal advice or equity allocation advice. It helps read the structure of the change so the person can decide what professional advice, conversation, or boundary is needed.
