# ChangeClear for Offer Decisions

Some career decisions are not just salary decisions. They are structure decisions.

Examples:

- Is a counter offer actually changing the original problem?
- Is a high salary offer buying pressure, commute, title risk, or future optionality?
- Is the safer option truly safer, or only more familiar?

ChangeClear does not make the decision for you and does not provide legal, financial, medical, or therapy advice. It helps clarify the pattern before acting.
