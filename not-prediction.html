ChangeClear Field Notes

This is not prediction.

ChangeClear is for moments when something is already changing and the user needs a clearer frame before acting.

It does not promise fate, guarantee an outcome, diagnose a person, or replace therapy, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or the user's own decision.

The product reads pattern, tension, timing, posture, and decision boundary. The value is not certainty. The value is acting with less fog.
