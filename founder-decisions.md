# ChangeClear for Founder Decisions

Founder decisions often look like product questions, but underneath they are change questions.

Examples:

- Is this feature request a roadmap signal or a strategic distraction?
- Did Product Hunt fail, or did it reveal which buyer moment is missing?
- Is 0 MRR a product problem, a distribution problem, or an offer problem?
- Before launch, has a real person trusted the product with a real question?

ChangeClear does not replace customer evidence, pricing tests, legal advice, financial advice, or founder judgment. It helps clarify the pattern before acting.
