ChangeClear Field Notes

When a customer request may rewrite the roadmap.

A customer willing to pay is not automatically a yes. The real question is whether the request creates reusable learning, or whether it turns the product into custom debt.

This is a ChangeClear moment because the decision is not only about a feature. It changes positioning, support burden, future sales, and the founder's default posture toward the market.

ChangeClear does not tell founders what to build. It helps read the pattern of the situation: what is pulling the company, what would become harder to reverse, and what boundary should be watched before acting.
