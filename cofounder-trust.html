When cofounder equity is really a trust change.

Cofounder tension often appears as a number: equity, title, salary, or decision rights. But the earlier change is usually trust, contribution, risk, and future conflict handling.

ChangeClear does not provide legal or equity advice. It helps read what is changing in the relationship before the numbers harden into a permanent structure.

A serious reading can make the decision boundary clearer: what would need to be true to continue, what would make the partnership more expensive than being solo, and what cannot be solved by another spreadsheet.
