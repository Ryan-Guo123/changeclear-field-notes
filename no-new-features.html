ChangeClear Field Notes

When adding features is avoiding distribution.

More building can feel like progress because it is controllable. But a first revenue problem is often not a product surface problem. It is an offer, trust, and buyer-moment problem.

The harder test is whether a stranger with a real decision will submit the question, read the free frame, and decide whether the paid half is worth unlocking.

ChangeClear uses that same test on itself: real question, free frame, paywall, checkout, and a daily review of where people drop off.
