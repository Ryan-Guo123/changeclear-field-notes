ChangeClear Field Notes

When an offer changes more than salary.

An offer can exchange money for commute, pressure, culture, title, learning curve, stability, and future option. A counter offer can change trust as much as compensation.

ChangeClear does not make career decisions for you. It helps read the structure of the change so the choice is not reduced to a single number.

A useful reading asks: what does this opportunity solve, what does it demand, and what will become harder to return to once you accept it?
