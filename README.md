# ChangeClear Field Notes

ChangeClear is a serious reading flow for moments of change.

It is built for situations where a person is not asking for a prediction, but trying to understand the structure of a decision before acting:

- founder decisions
- product direction and feature requests
- cofounder and partnership tension
- offer and career decisions
- relationship boundary shifts

ChangeClear is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or guaranteed prediction. It is structured guidance for reading the pattern of a situation.
