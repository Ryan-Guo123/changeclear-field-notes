# ChangeClear Method Boundary

ChangeClear is not trying to predict the future.

It is a structured reading flow for a specific situation:

- What is the question really asking?
- Which pattern is active?
- What is the tension?
- What direction does the situation support?
- What should be watched?
- What remains the user's decision?

ChangeClear is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, fate certainty, or guaranteed outcome.
